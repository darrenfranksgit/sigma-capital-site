Sigma Capital backs the technologies and platforms redefining surgical care — where AI, robotics, and data converge to improve outcomes at global scale.

Founded by Bjoern von Siemens, Sigma Capital builds on a 175-year family legacy in industrial engineering and healthcare technology to shape the next frontier of AI-native surgery.

Our Philosophy

Invest in Infrastructure

Sigma invests selectively across the surgical AI value chain, focusing on the infrastructure layers where long-term strategic advantage is created — including data capture, workflow integration, connectivity systems, and enabling robotics platforms.

Evergreen Ownership Model

As an evergreen investment platform, Sigma deploys capital flexibly across platform formation, growth-stage scaling, and strategic acquisition opportunities. Rather than operating within fixed fund cycles, Sigma takes ownership positions ranging from strategic minority stakes to control and co-control investments where infrastructure ownership is critical.

What Sets Us Apart

Deep Sector Expertise & Access

Backed by a European industrial family with global reach across software, automation, energy, and healthcare, Sigma Capital combines strong ties to leading academic institutions with access to key EU and US healthcare stakeholders. Beyond capital, Sigma brings clinical networks, regulatory expertise, operational know-how, leadership talent, and medtech distribution access.

Global Platform Architecture

With offices in Abu Dhabi, Berlin, Chicago, London, and San Francisco, Sigma unites Europe's industrial and clinical strength, US innovation and capital markets, Middle Eastern long-term capital, and Asia's manufacturing scale in one integrated global platform.

Proven Track Record

Sigma brings over 15 years of surgical and AI investing and operating experience, with over $300 million deployed across the ecosystem, including early positions in Intuitive Surgical and Auris Health, and the founding platform behind Caresyntax.
